I'm the CTO at ThisData, a security API which lets web apps protect their end-users from account takeover. I make things for the internet using Ruby on Rails, and keep the servers going with luck and google.

Things that push my buttons include: helping people solve their problems using my techy skills, cyber security, writing blog posts, and presenting at meetups and conferences.
